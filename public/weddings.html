How it works

Simple, custom, no pressure.

  1. Free consultation. We meet in person, by phone, or over Zoom to hear your vision and talk budget. No obligation, no upsell — just a real conversation about what you want your day to feel like.
  2. Custom proposal. We put together a plan and pricing built around what matters most to you. If centerpieces are your priority, we focus there. If you want a showstopping ceremony arch, we make that the moment.
  3. Design & delivery. On your day, we deliver and set up everything — bouquets at the suite, ceremony florals in place, centerpieces on tables — so it's all perfect before you arrive.
Pricing

Why budget-based pricing?

Because every couple is different. We'd rather build a design that fits your budget beautifully than hand you a fixed package that doesn't fit at all. Tell us what you're working with, and we'll make the most of every dollar.

What to bring to your consultation. A rough budget range, your venue (or a few you're considering), your colors and any inspiration photos you love, and a date or season. We'll take it from there.
