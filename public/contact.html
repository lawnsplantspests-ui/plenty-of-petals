What to expect

How a first conversation usually goes.

If you've never booked a florist before, the first call is the part we hear couples are most nervous about. Don't be — it's just a friendly conversation.

  1. Tell us the basics. Date, venue (or a few you're considering), rough budget, your colors. If you don't have all of that yet, no problem — we can help you think through it.
  2. Share what you love. Pinterest boards, Instagram saves, photos of weddings you've been to — whatever inspires you. We translate visual ideas into florals all the time.
  3. We'll send a proposal. Within a few days, you'll get a written breakdown of what we'd recommend and what it would cost. No pressure, no obligation. If it's not right, we adjust.
