Booking & consultations

Getting started.

How far in advance should I book my wedding florist?

The earlier the better — popular dates, especially spring and fall Saturdays in Central PA, book up well in advance. That said, don't assume a shorter timeline rules you out. If your date is still open on our calendar, we can often make it work. Reach out and ask — consultations are free.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes. Every consultation is free, with no obligation and no pressure. We can meet in person, by phone, or over Zoom — whatever is easiest for you. We'll talk through your vision, your venue, your colors, and your budget, then put together a custom proposal.

What should I bring to my consultation?

A rough budget range, your venue (or a few you're considering), your colors, any inspiration photos you love, and your date or season. Don't worry if you don't have it all figured out — that's what the conversation is for. Our guide to questions worth asking a wedding florist is a helpful read beforehand, too.

Can I make changes after booking?

Wedding plans evolve — guest counts shift, colors get tweaked, Pinterest boards grow. We're known for being flexible and working with you through changes as your day takes shape. Just keep us in the loop as early as you can.

Pricing

Budgets, not packages.

How does your pricing work? Do you have set packages?

We don't sell fixed packages. Every wedding is designed from scratch around your budget — we'd rather build something beautiful that fits what you're working with than hand you a package that doesn't. Tell us your budget range at the consultation and we'll show you how to make the most of it. Curious what different budgets actually buy? See our Central PA wedding flower budget breakdown.

What are DIY wedding flowers?

Our DIY option is bulk, wholesale-style blooms for couples who want to arrange their own flowers. You pick up at our Harrisburg studio a day or two before your event, hydrated and prepped so you can get straight to creating. It's a great fit for crafty couples and tighter budgets.

Logistics

The day itself.

Do you deliver and set up on the wedding day?

Yes. On your day we deliver and set up everything — bouquets to the suite, ceremony florals in place, centerpieces on the tables — so it's all perfect before you arrive. Day-of pickup of rented pieces can be arranged too.

Do you have a storefront I can visit?

We're a custom, appointment-only studio in Harrisburg, PA — not a walk-in flower shop. That's intentional: it keeps our overhead low and our focus entirely on your event. Consultations are scheduled at 717-608-1401 or through our contact page.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Harrisburg and all of Central Pennsylvania — including Camp Hill, Mechanicsburg, Hershey, Hummelstown, Middletown, Elizabethtown, Carlisle, and surrounding communities. See our full service area.

Do you do events besides weddings?

Yes. While weddings are our specialty, we create custom florals for showers, parties, celebrations of life, corporate events, and other occasions. See our event florals page — if it deserves flowers, we can design for it.
