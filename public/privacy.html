Privacy

Privacy Policy

Plenty of Petals is a small, appointment-only wedding and event florist in Harrisburg, PA. We collect only what we need to plan your flowers and stay in touch about your event. We do not sell or share your personal information.

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you call, text, or email us, we receive the contact details you choose to share — typically your name, phone number, email address, event date, venue, and a description of what you're looking for. If you book with us, we keep records of your contract, flower selections, and delivery details. When you visit our website, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may log standard request information such as your IP address, browser, and the pages you visited, which is used to keep the site secure and reliable.

How we use it

We use your information to follow up on consultations, prepare proposals, plan and execute your floral design, coordinate delivery logistics, and communicate about your event. We may send occasional seasonal updates about availability or studio news, but we do not send unsolicited marketing.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not rent or trade your contact details. We do not share your information with third parties for advertising. We do not run advertising trackers on this website.

Who we share with

We share only what's needed to do the work: payment processors handle payment information directly, and our website is hosted by Cloudflare. With your permission, we may coordinate with your venue, wedding planner, or photographer to ensure smooth delivery and setup.

Photos of your event

We may photograph our work at your event for portfolio and social media use. If you'd prefer we not post photos of your wedding or event, let us know at any time and we will remove them or refrain from posting.

How long we keep it

We keep client records (contracts, flower orders, invoices) for as long as needed for business and tax purposes. Consultation inquiries that don't lead to a booking are typically cleared within a year.

Cookies

This website does not set tracking cookies. Our hosting provider may use minimal technical cookies for security and performance.

Your choices

You can ask us at any time to delete your contact information, opt out of future follow-ups, or send you a copy of what we have on file. Just call, text, or email and we'll take care of it.
