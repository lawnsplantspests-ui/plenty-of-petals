How it works

Custom designed, beautifully delivered.

  1. Free consultation. We meet in person, by phone, or over Zoom to hear your vision and talk budget.
  2. Custom proposal. We put together a plan and pricing built around what matters most to you — not a generic package.
  3. Design & delivery. On your day, we deliver and set up so everything's perfect before you arrive.
Pricing, honestly

Why budget-based pricing?

Because every couple is different. We'd rather build a design that fits your budget beautifully than hand you a fixed package that doesn't fit at all. Tell us what you're working with, and we'll make the most of every dollar.
