Destination-level weddings

Florals that hold their own in a ballroom.

Hershey's venues lean formal — grand rooms, high ceilings, and a polish that rewards intentional, designed florals. That's a different design problem than a barn or a backyard, and it's one we love: statement centerpieces, structured ceremony pieces, and coordinated palettes that carry across a big room. For Hershey's garden and outdoor spots, we shift to heat-smart blooms and designs that photograph beautifully in open light.

Planning a Hershey wedding?

What to know about flowers here.

  1. Book early. Hershey dates fill fast — peak-season weekends can book a year or more out, and vendors follow suit. The earlier we talk, the more options you have.
  2. Match the room's scale. Formal venues swallow small arrangements. We design to your actual room so the flowers read beautifully from every seat.
  3. Coordinate with your venue. Resort venues run tight schedules for vendor access. We handle that coordination directly, so setup happens on time without involving you.
