Barn-country weddings

Designed for big, beautiful spaces.

Barn venues are gorgeous and demanding: high ceilings swallow small arrangements, and warm wood tones call for palettes that sing against them — blush, terracotta, dusty rose, burgundy. We design Hummelstown-area barn weddings with height, abundance, and loose garden style, so your flowers fill the space instead of disappearing into it.

  • Garden-style, hand-tied bouquets
  • Tall ceremony installations & freestanding arches
  • Farm-table runners with greens, candles & clustered blooms
  • Hanging & suspended florals where the venue allows
  • Delivery and full setup between Harrisburg and Hershey
Planning a barn wedding?

Three things we've learned.

  1. Scale up, not out. One tall, generous ceremony piece beats a dozen small touches in a barn — and often costs less than you'd think.
  2. Ask about hanging florals early. Suspended installations are showstoppers, but venues differ on what they allow. We'll coordinate directly with yours.
  3. Plan for the temperature. Barns run hot in summer and cool in shoulder season — we choose blooms that hold up either way.
